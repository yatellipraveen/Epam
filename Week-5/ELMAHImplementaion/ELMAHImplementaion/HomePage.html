Welcome EPAM

About

EPAM Systems, Inc. (also known as EPAM) is a global product development, digital platform engineering, and digital and product design agency with Fortune 1000 clients located around the world. The company’s industry expertise includes financial services, travel and consumer, software and hi-tech, business information and media, life sciences and healthcare, as well as other industries.

What We DO

EPAM has worked on projects in the financial services, healthcare, life sciences, media and entertainment, retail industries and more, including Liberty Global’s Horizon 4 platform, Memorial Sloan Kettering’s patient engagement mobile app [as part of their Teen and Young Adult Program (TYA@MSK)], and New Era Cap Company’s eCommerce website. For its traditional core offering, EPAM has long offered a full array of software development and product engineering services, describing itself as having “engineering DNA.” Through various acquisitions and investments in expansion, the company has added advanced technology, intelligent enterprise and digital engagement solutions, as well as design thinking and physical-digital capabilities to its portfolio. Additionally, EPAM has integrated its business, technology and engagement consulting practices into a consolidated offering referred to as EPAM Continuum.
